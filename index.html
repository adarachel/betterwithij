About

Ijeoma Okoro, DNP, APRN, has 15 years of diversified nursing experience in emergency medicine, urgent care, occupational health, and leadership roles. Her notable experience in high-acuity settings has given her an exceptional ability to assess, diagnose, and treat a wide range of illnesses while offering compassionate, patient-centered care.

As the owner of her own wellness practice, she combines her medical knowledge with an integrative approach to health and healing. She is passionate about preventive care, stress management, and alternatives to medication such as IV infusion therapy, mindfulness, and organic clean eating habits. With a thorough understanding of both traditional and alternative medicine, she encourages patients to take control of their health by developing customized wellness treatment plans which encourage whole-body wellness.

She brings clinical precision, leadership expertise, and an integrated strategy to her practice, and she is dedicated to bridging the gap between modern medicine and natural healing to help people achieve the health they deserve.
