About

Ijeoma Okoro, DNP, APRN, FNP-BC is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with 15 years cumulative experience in Nursing. Her accomplishments include working as an Emergency Room Nurse in a trauma hospital, an advanced practice nurse practitioner in a high-volume and fast-paced Urgent Care setting and three years of leadership experience in Occupational Health and Wellness Management.

Better With IJ PLLC's mission is to be an NP-led practice at the forefront of providing accessible, personalized, and evidence-based wellness solutions in improving community health outcomes.

Services offered include acute and chronic disease management, preventative care, wellness coaching, on-site testing for flu, cold symptoms, UTI screening designing corporate wellness programs.
