Better With IJ Kicks Off 2026 With Purpose, Passion, and a Reintroduction to
Community Care

The start of a new year always brings a sense of renewal — an invitation to align purpose with action. For Better With IJ Wellness Practice, 2026 begins with more than resolutions; it’s a reintroduction of our mission: to make healthcare simple, accessible, and deeply human.

This January, our team kicked off the year not only by reopening our clinic doors but by stepping outside them by collaborating with other health professionals — volunteering at the Chevron Houston Marathon as part of the medical support team.


Serving the Community, One Runner at a Time

While thousands of runners pushed through miles of sweat, triumph, and determination, our clinicians worked tirelessly behind the scenes — assessing injuries, managing dehydration, and helping participants recover without needing a hospital visit.

From the early morning chill to the last runner crossing the finish line, the Better With IJ medical team exemplified what community health truly means: showing up where people need care the most.

Our goal was simple — provide immediate, compassionate medical support so participants could be treated, stabilized, and discharged safely, rather than facing unnecessary emergency room visits.

Why We Do What We Do

At Better With IJ, we believe wellness shouldn’t be complicated or restricted by insurance boundaries. That’s why our clinic proudly offers a Direct Pay Model — transparent pricing and flexible wellness plans designed to make care affordable for everyone.

No insurance? No problem.

We’ve created a model that puts you first — offering preventive visits, wellness consultations, and IV hydration therapy at prices that make sense for working families and individuals without coverage.

This isn’t just a financial model — it’s a movement toward empowered health choices.

A Fresh Look, A Familiar Mission

As we reintroduce our clinic this year, we’re doing so with renewed energy and visibility. Step into our modern, wellness-inspired space — where preventive medicine meets mindfulness, and every patient encounter starts with compassion.

Our goal for 2026: to serve more families, expand community outreach, and continue building Better With IJ into a trusted hub for holistic, nurse practitioner-led care.

Looking Ahead

January’s marathon reminded us why we started: to bridge the gap between clinical expertise and compassionate care. As we move through 2026, you’ll see more of us in your community — from volunteer events to wellness workshops, each reflecting our guiding principle: Health is not a luxury; it’s a lifestyle.

If you’re ready to take control of your wellness this year, visit us at Better With IJ Wellness Practice — where healthcare meets heart.
