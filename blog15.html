Heart and Hustle:
How to Protect Your Heart While
Chasing Your Dreams

Because your heart works hard for you — how are you caring for it?

February is a month filled with love, hustle, and heart — in every sense of the word. While we often focus on giving our hearts to others, it’s just as important to give it the care it deserves.

At Better With IJ Wellness Practice, we see heart health as more than a medical metric — it’s a measure of how well you’re balancing your ambition with intention. Whether you’re an entrepreneur, parent, student, or caregiver, your heart keeps up with your pace. This month, we’re helping you protect it.

Know Your Numbers: Lipid Screening & Prevention

The first step in caring for your heart is knowing your baseline. A simple lipid panel can reveal important details about your cholesterol, triglycerides, and overall cardiovascular risk.

We recommend:

  • Annual lipid screening starting at age 20 (or earlier with risk factors).
  • Blood pressure checks every 3–6 months.
  • Routine assessments for blood sugar, diet, and stress levels.

Heart disease often begins silently. Prevention is the best form of protection.

Balance the Hustle with Self-Care

It’s easy to equate productivity with success — but your body needs recovery as much as it needs drive. Small, daily habits can dramatically reduce your heart risk:

  • Move your body for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Choose heart-healthy meals rich in omega-3s, fiber, and greens.
  • Hydrate well — your heart loves oxygen-rich, hydrated blood.
  • Unplug from screens before bed and aim for 7–8 hours of rest.
  • Breathe intentionally — stress management starts with oxygen.

Your hustle matters. But so does your heartbeat.
