Is Your Healthcare Provider A Good Fit For You?

Receiving high-quality care depends on selecting the right healthcare provider. At Better With IJ Mindful Practice, we collaborate with skilled medical professionals to identify the best course of action for your care. Knowing how to choose the best fit for your health needs might be overwhelming and our goal is to ease the burden.


By providing holistic, patient-centered care that integrates preventive, functional, and evidence-based medicine to enhance general health and well-being, a healthcare provider hopes to leave their patients in a healthier condition than when they began their wellness journey.

Here are several approaches to determine whether your healthcare provider is a good fit for your health concerns:



  • 1. IDENTIFY YOUR NEEDS
    • Incorporate a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables to ensure you’re getting a wide range of vitamins and minerals.
    • Determine what type of provider you want? Medical Doctors, Nurse Practitioners or Physician Assistants are trained to provide a broad range of medical services. They can diagnose, treat, and prescribe medications for certain conditions.
    • Do you want a primary care provider (PCP), specialist, or holistic practitioner.

  • 2. CHECK CREDENTIALS AND EXPERIENCE
    • Confirm the provider's education, credentials, and licensure.
    • Consider board certification, experience, and specialization in your area of interest.

  • 3. READ REVIEWS AND ASK FOR RECOMMENDATIONS
    • Research online reviews (Google, Healthgrades, Zocdoc, etc.)
    • Seek referrals from reputable sources, such as family, friends, and colleagues.

  • 4. CONFIRM INSURANCE AND PAYMENT OPTIONS
    • Confirm if the provider accepts your insurance or provides cash payment choices.
    • Inquire about out-of-pocket costs for appointments, labs, and treatments.

  • 5. EVALUATE COMMUNICATION AND APPROACH
    • Schedule a first consultation to examine communication style.
    • Ensure your concerns are heard and treatment alternatives are fully explained.

  • 6. CONSIDER CONVENIENCE AND ACCESSIBILITY
    • Confirm location, office hours, and appointment availability.
    • Check if they provide telemedicine or concierge services for flexibility.

  • 7. EVALUATE THEIR TREATMENT PHILOSOPHY
    • If seeking holistic techniques, inquire about their use of diet, mindfulness, IV therapy, or other treatments.
    • Ensure their philosophy fits your health and wellbeing goals.
